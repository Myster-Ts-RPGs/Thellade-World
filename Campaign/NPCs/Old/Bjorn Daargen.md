## Goals

### Safety & Security
Bjorn's primary goal is to ensure the safety and security of the town of Feldon. He strives to maintain law and order, protecting the citizens from any threats that may arise. Bjorn also aims to strengthen the bond between the townsfolk and the guards, fostering trust and a sense of security within the community.

### Justice
Bjorn is motivated by his deep sense of duty and the desire to uphold justice. He seeks to protect the innocent, maintain a peaceful environment, and ensure that the town of Feldon thrives. Bjorn's motivation is driven by his own experiences, having witnessed the consequences of lawlessness and the importance of maintaining a strong defense.

## History

Bjorn's mixed heritage gave him a unique perspective on the world, allowing him to bridge gaps between different cultures and build connections. As he grew older, Bjorn discovered a passion for martial training, particularly in swordplay and combat tactics.

Bjorn's dedication and natural leadership qualities caught the attention of [[Kamderah]]'s town guards, and he quickly rose through the ranks. With his exceptional combat skills, strategic thinking, and ability to diffuse tense situations, Bjorn was recruited by [[Rosnik Grimtor]] and was appointed as the Guard Captain of [[Meshwich]].

In his role as Guard Captain, Bjorn has implemented reforms to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of the town's guard force. He held the respect and trust of the townsfolk, who rely on his leadership and guidance to ensure their safety. Bjorn's vigilant presence and dedication to duty made him an essential figure in maintaining the peace and stability of [[Meshwich]].
