### Ancestroy Home

## History

Inspired by the stories of Meshwich's former glory, Harbin took it upon himself to rebuild the town from its ruins. With unwavering determination and a clear vision, he rallied a group willing settlers to join his cause.

Through hard work, perseverance, and the aid of those who believed in his vision, Harbin oversaw the construction of homes, the establishment of farms, and the rebuilding of key infrastructure. He led Meshwich in crafting a strong community, centered aroundfarming, harmony, and a shared sense of purpose.

Harbin's leadership and guidance created a spirit of unity among the townsfolk, fostering a deep respect and reverence for him as the founder and first Jarl of Meshwich.
