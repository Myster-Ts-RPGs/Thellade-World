## History

Rosnik Grimtor was born into a respected dwarven family with a long history within financing. From a young age, he was trained in the arts of finance and administration, honing his skills in leadership and management. As he grew older, Rosnik distinguished himself through his meticulous attention to detail and his unwavering dedication.

Rosnik heard of the settlers making their claim to [[Meshwich]] and found a way to make a new for himself. After becoming close with [[Harbin Wester]] he entrusted with the position of Steward due to his exceptional qualifications and deep knowledge. Since assuming the role, he has worked tirelessly to ensure the efficient operation of the town's infrastructure and services.

Rosnik's expertise extends to matters of trade and commerce, overseeing the markets and negotiating with merchants to secure the best deals for Meshwich. He is known for his fair and just decision-making, earning the respect and trust of the townsfolk.

As Steward, Rosnik acts as a trusted advisor to the Jarl, providing guidance on matters of governance and policy. He works closely with other town officials, guilds, and organizations to foster cooperation and maintain a stable and prosperous Meshwich.
