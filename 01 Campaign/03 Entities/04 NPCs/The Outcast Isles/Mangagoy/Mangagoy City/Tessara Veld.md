## Overview
Tessara Veld is one of the 44 enchanted stewards of [[Whisperwind Suites]], a half-elf, half-gnome woman with alert eyes and unmatched memory. She oversees guest check-ins and movement with precision, standing silently beside the front desk until summoned with exact phrasing. She wears the steward uniform like armor and tracks who enters Room 7 without writing anything down.
