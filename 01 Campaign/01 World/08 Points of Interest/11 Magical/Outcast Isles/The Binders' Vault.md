## Overview
### Description
> Hidden deep beneath the Hollow Chamber in central Boba, **The Binder’s Vault** is a sealed arcane structure built to contain one of the mouths of Xal'Zul—part of the cryptic Third Verse. Accessible only through concealed sublayers within the city’s old civic tunnels, the Vault radiates divine suppression and abyssal pressure. The surrounding stone is etched with sigil wards older than the Pre-Rising, many now dimmed or fractured.

### Purpose
> Serves as one of the key binding sites for the fractured deity Xal’Zul. The Vault specifically seals the Third Verse—preventing the reformation or awakening of a mouth fragment buried in the city's core.

### Access
> Entry requires traversal through restricted vault corridors below the Hollow Chamber. Multiple illusory failsafes and sigil-based misdirection protect the true path.

### Current Status
> **Actively guarded** by members of the **Crimson Shroud**, who maintain the outer seals and repel any incursion—arcane, divine, or profane. Internal wards remain intact but are weakening under Verse resonance.

### Known Events
- Believed to be the **oldest surviving Pre-Rising structure** in Boba
- Resurfaced in arcane readings following the collapse of Deepmark Sanctum
- Minor cult activity has been detected near the Vault’s outer bounds
